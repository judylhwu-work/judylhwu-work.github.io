Smart Huddles

External-facing product for healthcare teams to build surveys, collect responses, and run structured huddle processes. I'm the sole designer on the in-app huddle tool end to end.

Role Product Designer (Sole designer)
Duration 2024 — Present
Team PM, Engineering, Customer Success
Tools Figma

Overview

Most of LeanTaaS's product work is internal — tools built for hospital ops teams, capacity planners, and administrators. Smart Huddles is different: it's an external-facing product that healthcare teams use directly to run structured daily huddle processes.

The core workflow is a survey builder and response collector. Team leads build custom surveys, distribute them to their staff, and collect responses as part of a structured check-in process. The goal is to help healthcare teams surface problems early, track progress over time, and create a culture of continuous improvement — without adding administrative burden.

My Role

I own the builder and response modules end to end. The builder is where team leads create and configure their surveys — choosing question types, setting logic, and previewing how responses will be collected. The response module is the experience staff members see when they fill one out.

Working as the sole designer means I'm involved across the full lifecycle: discovery with PMs and customer success, wireframes, high-fidelity designs, design QA, and iteration based on customer feedback from pilot teams. There's no design handoff — I stay with the work until it's in production and performing.

Impact

Smart Huddles has become a key selling point for new customers. Pilot teams are measurably improving — tracking metrics that weren't visible before and closing the loop between leadership goals and frontline action. The in-app tool gives the huddle process structure and accountability it didn't have when it was running through spreadsheets and ad hoc check-ins.
